Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ghost Mode! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website. By using the Sound Threads website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information from you when you use our website, including:

  • Personal Information: We collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, shipping address, etc.) when you create an account, place an order, or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with our website, such as which products you view, what you search for, and how you navigate through our site. This data helps us improve the website's features and functionality.
  • Device Information: We collect non-personal information related to your device, including browser type, operating system, and device settings, to optimize your experience on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To Process Transactions: We use your information to process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and providing customer support.
  • To Improve Our Website: Usage data helps us enhance the website's features and functionality, ensuring it meets the needs of our customers.
  • To Provide Personalized Recommendations: We may use your browsing and purchase history to offer personalized product recommendations.
  • To Communicate with You: If you opt in to our newsletter, we may send updates about new products, special offers, or promotions.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We value your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also share information if required by law, such as to comply with a legal process or regulatory requirement.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Your Choices

You can control how we use your information in the following ways:

  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can unsubscribe from email notifications and newsletters at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email or updating your communication preferences in your account settings.
  • Update or Delete Your Account: You can update your profile information or request to delete your account at any time through your account settings or by contacting our customer support.
  • Managing Cookies: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that some features of the website may not function properly without cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Sound Threads is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
